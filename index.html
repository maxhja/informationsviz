What is this?

This is an interactive web-app for filtering out intresting stocks by using key ratio

What is what?

The box in the top left corner is a Index of all industry groups in sweden, click it if you want to see which companys who are in that group

The box in the top right corner shows the a stocks value over time

The box shows parallell coordinates of all industry groups in sweden. You can filter the Industry groups or Stocks by dragging them up and down.

Explanation of key ratio

Beta A measure of the volatility, or systematic risk, of a security

EV/EBIT used to measure a company's return on investment

Current PE Definition of 'Price-Earnings Ratio - P/E Ratio'

PS Definition of 'Price-To-Sales Ratio - PSR'

EV/Invested Capital Definition of 'Enterprice value / Invested capital'

Dividned Yield shows how much a company pays out in dividends each year relative to its share price
